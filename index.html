About Me

Being a Full Stack Developer is my second major career. I have spent the past 20 years as a Montessori teacher. Most of my experience was working with gifted students who also had one or more learning differences. This is a challenging career day to day and I knew it was time to retire and find a new passion. My teaching career honed my patience, creativity and ability to work with almost any personality type. These great transferable skills are helping me in my new endeavour. I have two grown sons who prefer me not to share their photos and so my down time is spent walking Wrinkles, my six year old Frenchie, as he is my constant shadow.
